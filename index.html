FULL ISLAND TOUR

The full island tour starts at your accommodation and heads to the North West Point of the island, with its first stop at the California Lighthouse, then the Goldmine Ruins, alongside the scenic North Coast to the Natural Bridge, then the Ayo Rock Formation, and through the French Man Pass on to the South East Point.

Here we drive by the Red Anchor, where you may see some wild donkeys. After that, we drop at the famous Baby Beach for a 30-minute swim. Before heading back to your accommodation, we will drive by Mangel Halto Beach and the Balashi Brewery.

Fee: per person for adults: $79.00 and for children (under 15 years): $68.00. Tax is included.

Duration: 4 – 4 ½ hours Pick-up time: 2:15 PM.
 Bring towels, swimming gear, sneakers, sun protection and cash. The tour includes a bottle of water.
